Dr Myles Munroe

An influential minister and evangelist gone too soon

Myles Munroe, OBE (20 April 1954 - 9 November 2014) was a Bahamian evangelist and ordained pastor who created and directed the Bahamas Faith Ministries International (BFMI) and Myles Munroe International. He was also an author, lecturer, and leadership consultant. He was the president of the International Leadership Training Institute and the chief executive officer and chairman of the board of the International Third World Leaders Association, as well as the author of numerous books. On November 9, 2014, Munroe and his wife died in a plane crash. Officials from the Bahamas said their plane collided with a crane at a shipyard near Grand Bahama International Airport.
